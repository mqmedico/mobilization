Dashboard

Monitor our mobilization progress

Guidelines & Information

    THINGS YOU CAN CHECK/MONITOR ON THE DASHBOARD:
  • Your own submitted invitees/attendees.
    1. Search for your name in the "INVITED BY"column
    2. Then click on "ONLY" that will appear beside it
    3. All your submitted invitees will be displayed in the table on the right panel
  • Submission progress per cell group or life group networks.
  • Invitess/Attendees submitted under your selected CGN or LGN will be displayed in the "INVITATION SUMMARY"
  • Monitor the overall progress by checking out the "ATTENDEES VS. TARGET"box.

If you have any questions, comments, suggestions or clarifications about the Dashboard below,
please contact your LG leader or CG leader.
