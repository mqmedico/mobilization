Mobilization Form

Submit the names of your invitees online.

Guidelines

  • Write your Membership ID & MOBILE NUMBER in the "INVITED BY" box everytime you register your invitee/s.
  • Checkout the Target Summary page to know the target for your LIFE GROUP NETWORK.
  • List your invitees under your respective LIFE GROUP NETWORKS.
  • For each invitee, please provide Full Names, Contact Number, Location, Age (if below 13 years old only)
  • Please don't forget to register yourself as one of your INVITEEs once and specify if you are joining On-site or FB Live.

For more information in submitting the names of your invitees,
please contact your Life Group Leader.

If you have any questions, click on "Need Help?"
